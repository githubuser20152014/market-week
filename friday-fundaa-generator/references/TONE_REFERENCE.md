# Vibe Check Tone Reference

The Friday Fundaa must sound like a conversation between a cynical macro strategist and a friend at a bar. High signal, zero fluff, sharp edges.

## Key Phrases & One-Liners
- "That’s cute." (Dismissing obvious or small news)
- "The decoy." / "The silent assassin." / "The extortion."
- "If you aren't hedged, you aren't paying attention."
- "Peak 2026."
- "The Peace Dividend is dead."
- "Welcome to the era of the $X,XXX geopolitical surcharge."
- "Not financial advice. Just vibes and cold, hard signals."

## Emoji Usage
- 🔥 (General chaos/hot topic)
- 🚢 (Shipping/Trade)
- 🛢️ (Energy/Oil)
- ⛽ (Gas pump)
- 🛒 (Consumer goods/Groceries)
- ✈️ (Travel/Flights)

## Examples

### The "Tip of the Iceberg" Hook
> "The Strait of Hormuz is effectively closed, Brent crude is screaming at $126, and everyone is crying over a $4.11 gallon of gas. That’s the decoy. While the news cycle obsesses over naval demining, your credit card is taking a systemic hit that goes way beyond your commute."

### The "Wealth Impact" Format (LinkedIn)
> **The Signal:** The UAE officially terminated its 60-year OPEC membership today, choosing "agility" over Saudi-led quotas.
> 
> **The Wealth Impact:** The cartel is dead. Expect a permanent layer of structural volatility in global crude pricing as the UAE operates as a high-volume free agent.
