# Friday Fundaa Structure Reference

Each Friday Fundaa should follow this multi-platform template to ensure consistency and maximum reach.

## 1. Substack Article (Core)
- **Title**: Punchy, dollar-focused, highlighting the visible vs. hidden contrast.
- **Intro**: "Huh, didn't know that" moment or a sharp "Vibe Check."
- **The "Math"**: 3-4 bulleted points breaking down macro costs into household dollars (e.g., The Commute Tax, The Logistics Surcharge).
- **The Mechanism**: 1-2 paragraphs explaining the global "plumbing" (why X leads to Y).
- **The Vibe/Bottom Line**: Cynical summary + "Not financial advice" disclaimer.
- **Footer**: Links to other editions + standard educational disclaimer.

## 2. LinkedIn Post
- **Formatting**: Use the "Vibe Check" Signal/Impact/Vibe format.
- **Emoji-Heavy**: Lead with 1-2 primary emojis (e.g., 🔥, 🚢).
- **Call to Action**: Direct to Substack link in comments.

## 3. X (Thread)
- **Hooks**: Lead with the most shocking dollar figure.
- **Thread Count**: 5-7 tweets.
- **Visuals**: Describe or include charts/emojis.

## 4. WhatsApp Blurb
- **Length**: 150-250 words.
- **Summary**: Punchy list of 3 "wallet hits."
- **Edge**: Must feel like a "shared secret" or an urgent wake-up call.
