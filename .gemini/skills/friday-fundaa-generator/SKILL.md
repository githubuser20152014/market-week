# Friday Fundaa Generator

This skill automates the creation of the "Friday Fundaa" newsletter, transforming dry macro news into "Real Dollar Terms" wake-up calls for the reader.

## Workflow

1.  **News Survey**: Survey recent news digests (e.g., from `news-digest/`) to identify high-resonance topics (e.g., supply shocks, energy, macro shifts).
2.  **Topic Selection**: Propose 2-3 topics with a specific "wallet impact" angle.
3.  **Core Content Creation**: Draft the Substack article using the "Vibe Check" tone.
    -   **Hook**: Use a specific, personally relevant dollar figure in the title.
    -   **Contrast**: Highlight the "Tip of the Iceberg" (visible vs. systemic cost).
    -   **Tone**: Cynical, witty, irreverent, finance-doomer energy. Use emojis (🔥, 🚢, 🛢️).
4.  **Social Variations**: Generate tailored posts for LinkedIn (Signal/Impact/Vibe), X (Thread), and WhatsApp (substantial blurb).

## Tonal Guidelines (The "Vibe Check")

-   **Unmistakable Humanity**: Avoid AI-sounding fluff. Use punchy one-liners ("If you aren't hedged, you aren't paying attention", "Peak 2026").
-   **Aggressive Transparency**: Frame structural economic pain as "cold, hard signals."
-   **Representative Math**: Bridge the gap between abstract news and household impact (e.g., "$0.90/gal = $700/year").
