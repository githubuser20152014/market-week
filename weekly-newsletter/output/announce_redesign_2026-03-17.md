# The Morning Brief just got better. Here's what changed.

Starting tomorrow, the Morning Brief will have a new personality.

The tables are gone. No more US Market Close grid, no Overnight Markets breakdown, no Currencies & Safe Havens rows. Gone.

Here's why.

Those tables were a data dump. Raw numbers without a story. You could get that from any finance app — and you probably already have three of them on your phone. That's not what this is for.

What you actually need at 8 AM is: *what happened, why it happened, what it means for your portfolio, and what to watch today.* In plain English. No terminal required.

So that's what the brief is now. One continuous narrative — the scene, the context, the signal, and the action. Same data underneath. Better wrapper.

The structure going forward:

- **The Brief** — what moved, why, and what it means for you
- **Market-Moving Headlines** — 5 links worth your time
- **Positioning Notes** — specific, actionable

That's it. Shorter. More useful. Easier to read on your phone at the open.

If you want the raw numbers, they're always at [frameworkfoundry.info](https://frameworkfoundry.info).

See you in the morning.
