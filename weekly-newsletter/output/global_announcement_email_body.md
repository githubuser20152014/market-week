Starting this Saturday, there's only one weekly edition.

We've been publishing two — a US edition and an International edition — and asking you to read both if you wanted the full picture. That never made much sense. Markets don't have editions. A move in the yen affects the Nasdaq. European equity strength tells you something about US sector rotation. What happens in China is in your ETF whether you follow it or not.

So we merged them.

The new **Framework Foundry Weekly — Global Investor Edition** covers it all in one read:

- What the week's dominant macro theme was, and why it matters
- US, European, and Asia-Pacific equities — compared, not siloed
- Currency markets and what dollar moves mean for your portfolio
- Commodities and metals
- Economic events — what happened and what's next
- Positioning notes that reflect the full global picture

Same Saturday cadence. One email instead of two. A narrative that actually connects the dots across markets.

The old US and International editions are still in the archive at frameworkfoundry.info if you want to look back at them. But going forward, it's Global.
