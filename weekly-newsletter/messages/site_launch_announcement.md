## Email / Subscriber version

**Subject: We rebuilt the site — and it's worth a look**

If you haven't visited frameworkfoundry.info lately, now's a good time.

We added something we've wanted for a while: **Market IQ** — a library of 36 flashcard-style explainers covering the indicators we reference every week. CPI, NFP, the yield curve, Fed funds rate, PCE... each one broken down in plain English with context on what the current reading actually means for you. You can search, filter by category, or just browse the A–Z index. It's the kind of reference we wish existed when we started paying attention to macro.

There's also a new editorial series called **Friday Fundaa** — short, focused pieces that take one economic concept and explain why it matters *right now*. The first one covers stagflation. More coming.

The homepage got a proper layout too — Weekly, Daily, Market IQ, and Fundaa each have their own tab, so nothing gets lost.

The newsletter hasn't changed. But the site it lives on finally feels like it belongs.
