VXUS and IXUS both cover the same territory: every publicly traded company outside the US. That's roughly 7,500–8,000 stocks across developed and emerging markets.

The key question isn't which fund — it's how much. Most research suggests 20–40% international allocation for long-term investors, though US-centric portfolios have outperformed over the past decade.
