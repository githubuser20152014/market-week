VTI tracks the entire US market — over 3,600 stocks including small and mid caps. VOO tracks only the S&P 500 — the 500 largest US companies. Both charge 0.03% expense ratio.

In practice, performance has been nearly identical over any 10-year period. The choice usually comes down to whether you want small-cap exposure.
