Sector ETFs let you overweight specific parts of the economy — technology (XLK), healthcare (XLV), energy (XLE), and more. Used thoughtfully, they can tilt your portfolio toward macro themes.

The risk: you're making a concentrated bet. If you already own VTI, adding XLK means you're doubling down on tech. Know what you already own before adding sectors.
